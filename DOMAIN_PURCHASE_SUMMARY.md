## Domain Name Suggestions

**Best Options:**
- `expirycare.in` - Direct, clear, Indian TLD
- `nevermiss.in` - Matches your project name
- `expirycare.com` - International, professional
- `lifeadmin.in` - Descriptive
- `trackexpiry.in` - Action-oriented

**Domain Selection Tips:**
- ✅ Keep it short (under 15 characters)
- ✅ Easy to spell and remember
- ✅ Use `.in` for Indian audience, `.com` for global
- ❌ Avoid hyphens and numbers
- ✅ Check social media handle availability

## Purchase Steps (Namecheap Example)

1. **Visit Namecheap:**
   - Go to [namecheap.com](https://www.namecheap.com)
   - Click "Sign Up" (top right)

2. **Create Account:**
   - Enter email and password
   - Verify email

3. **Search Domain:**
   - Enter your desired domain name
   - Check availability
   - Select domain extension (.com, .in, etc.)

4. **Add to Cart:**
   - Click "Add to Cart"
   - Review pricing
   - Select registration period (1 year minimum)

5. **Checkout:**
   - Review order
   - Choose payment method (UPI/Card)
   - Complete payment

6. **Domain Management:**
   - Domain will appear in your account
   - Enable auto-renewal
   - Enable privacy protection (if free)

## Important Settings After Purchase

- [ ] Enable auto-renewal (prevents domain loss)
- [ ] Enable privacy protection (hides your contact info)
- [ ] Unlock domain (if you might transfer later)
- [ ] Verify email address
- [ ] Save login credentials securely

## Next Steps After Purchase

1. **Note Your Domain:**
   - Write down your domain name
   - Save registrar login details

2. **Proceed to Deployment:**
   - Follow `DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md` → Section 2
   - Add domain to Vercel
   - Configure DNS records

3. **Update Project Files:**
   - Update `public/robots.txt` (replace `yourdomain.in`)
   - Update `app/sitemap.ts` (replace `yourdomain.in`)
   - Update environment variable `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` (if used)
